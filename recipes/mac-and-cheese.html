Baked Mac and Cheese

Description

This Southern style Mac and Cheese is great for for Thanksgiving, family gatherings, or any event when you're craving a homemade delicious mac and cheese. Easy to make and uses 4 different cheeses for a unique mac and cheese experiences. Sharp cheddar and gruyere for falvor and melty fontina for consistency, its then topped off with parmesan for an extra bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb elbow macaroni
  • 1 lb sharp cheddar
  • 8 oz fontina
  • 8 oz gruyere
  • 2 oz parmesan
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 2 eggs
  • paprika
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • black pepper
  • cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Grate all of your cheese.
  2. Boil pasta to al dente.
  3. Drain and return to the pot.
  4. Mix in butter and cheese (saving roughly 2 oz of each and all of the parmesan for later).
  5. Add milk and spices to taste.
  6. Beat eggs and add once pasta is cool to avoid scrambling.
  7. Move into baking dish and top with remaining cheese.
  8. Bake at 375 degress for 45 minutes
