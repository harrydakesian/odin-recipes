Easy Homemade Chili

Description

Easy to make homemade chili with ground beef and beans thats perfect to warm you up on a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) can kidney beans
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can stewed tomatoes
  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and onion in a saucepan over medium heat until meat is brown and onion is tender.
  2. Stir in tomato sauce, kidney beans, stewed tomatoes, and water.
  3. Add seasonings.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, cover and let simmer for 30 minutes.
